FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

Pressure washing uses high-force water to blast away surface dirt — effective for hard surfaces like concrete and brick, but damaging on softer materials. Soft washing uses low pressure combined with biodegradable cleaning solutions to safely treat roofs, siding, stucco, and wood. WashPro uses the right method for every surface.

Yes. We use soft wash on all delicate surfaces — stucco, painted siding, roofing materials, and solar panels. Our process is ARMA-compliant for roofs and uses purified water on solar panels. We've cleaned thousands of homes across the SFV without a single damage claim.

Most exterior washes last 1–3 years depending on local conditions, tree coverage, and surface type. Roof soft washes are backed by our 5-year warranty — if growth returns within that window, we come back at no charge. Solar panel cleanings are recommended every 6–12 months for peak efficiency.

Absolutely. We use biodegradable, eco-certified cleaning solutions that break down safely after application. We take extra care to pre-wet and post-rinse any landscaping near the work area. Once the surface dries, it's completely safe for children, pets, and plants.

Most residential jobs are completed in 2–5 hours. A single-story house wash typically takes 2–3 hours. Roof cleaning and full property packages run 3–6 hours. We give you a time estimate with your quote so you know exactly what to expect.

Pricing is based on property size, surface type, level of buildup, and the services requested. We provide free, no-obligation quotes — either over the phone or with a quick on-site assessment. No hidden fees, no surprise charges. The price you're quoted is the price you pay.
